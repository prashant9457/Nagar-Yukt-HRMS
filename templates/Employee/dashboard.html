Frequently Asked Questions
How do I apply for leave?

Navigate to Leave Management section, fill out the leave application form with the required details including leave type, dates, and reason. Submit the request for manager approval.

Where can I find my payslips?

Go to the Payroll section to view and download your current and historical payslips. You can download them as PDF documents.

How do I update my personal information?

Contact the HR department or submit a request through the support ticket system to update your personal information in the system.
